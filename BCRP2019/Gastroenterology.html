Once your patient has charity care, please contact the Emerging Referrals Manager.  The Emerging referrals manager will give you directions on how to schedule your patient  for their specialty referral appointment.

Cost

Cost to EHHOP Patient: Cost to the patient varies, though patients with CCL1 usually pay $15.

Cost to Society: Outpatient Gastroenterology appointments in New York cost ~$300 out-of-pocket.

Appointment costs by charity care level are as follows:

  • CCL1 - $15
  • CCL2 - $18
  • CCL3 - $36
  • CCL4 - $90
  • CCL5 - $108
  • CCL6 - $135
  • CCL7 - $180

Wait Time

Wait time will vary depending on procedure.
