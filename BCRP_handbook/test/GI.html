Cost

Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their income, Financial Services will  grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are  placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1, a symptomatic colonoscopy will cost  $150, and an EGD will cost $120. To find out the costs for patients who are  not Level 1, please contact the Accessory Referrals Manager, Kapil Mishra.

Wait time

How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services (on Average this takes 20 days). Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 2 weeks after a patient has received charity care for a podiatry appointment to be scheduled.
